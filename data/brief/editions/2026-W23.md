## item-1 — UC Riverside opens AI/high-tech research partnership with the Navy's Corona warfare center

### What happened
UC Riverside and the U.S. Naval Surface Warfare Center, Corona Division announced an education and research partnership spanning high-tech and AI-adjacent work. The agreement formalizes joint research and a talent pipeline between the campus and the federal defense lab.

### Why it matters to UC
A federal defense research relationship raises responsible-use and oversight questions that UC answers at the systemwide level but that UCR's own baseline does not yet cover: UCOP maintains systemwide Responsible AI Principles, while the baseline records no campus AI council at UC Riverside. New defense-tied AI research lands at a campus without a local governance body to steward it.

### For the committee
Should UCR stand up a campus AI council before scaling federally-funded AI research, or is systemwide oversight sufficient for partnerships of this kind?
