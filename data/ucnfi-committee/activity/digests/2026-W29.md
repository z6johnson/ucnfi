## By topic

### Publications (papers, preprints)

- [goldberg-k] "GaP: A Graph-as-Policy Multi-Agent Self-Learning Harness For Variational Automation Tasks" — arXiv, 2026-07-06 — OA-7
  Proposes a multi-agent framework that combines interpretable robot programming with model-free adaptability to handle objects with variable geometry and pose in commercial and industrial settings.

- [goldberg-k] "RoboVista: Evaluating Vision Language Models for Diverse Robot Applications" — arXiv, 2026-07-06 — OA-7
  Introduces a benchmark for assessing how well vision-language models support robotic reasoning across different embodiments, visual conditions, and planning demands, including agriculture and industry use cases.

- [vigna-g] "SeedSmith: LLM-Driven Seed Synthesis for Directed Fuzzing" — arXiv, 2026-07-09 — OA-1, OA-4
  Uses LLMs to generate semantically meaningful fuzzing seeds that address two known failure modes in directed fuzzing — incomplete static analysis of indirect calls and poor crash-path guidance — to improve vulnerability discovery.
