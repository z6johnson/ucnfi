## By topic

### Publications (papers, preprints)

- [goldberg-k] "GaP: A Graph-as-Policy Multi-Agent Self-Learning Harness For Variational Automation Tasks" — arXiv, 2026-07-06 — OA-7
  Proposes a multi-agent framework that combines interpretable robot programming with model-free adaptability for commercial and industrial automation tasks with high object-geometry and pose variation.

- [goldberg-k] "RoboVista: Evaluating Vision Language Models for Diverse Robot Applications" — arXiv, 2026-07-06 — OA-7
  Introduces a benchmark for evaluating VLMs across varied robot embodiments, visual conditions, and planning demands, with a modular focus on individual decision components.

- [vigna-g] "SeedSmith: LLM-Driven Seed Synthesis for Directed Fuzzing" — arXiv, 2026-07-09 — OA-1, OA-4
  Uses LLMs to generate semantically meaningful seeds for directed fuzzers, targeting two failure modes — incomplete static analysis of indirect calls and lack of crash-oriented semantic guidance — that limit vulnerability discovery in long fuzzing campaigns.
